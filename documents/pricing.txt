# Pricing Guide

## Subscription Plans
ABC Technologies offers three subscription plans:
1. **Basic Plan**: $29/month. Includes core business management features and standard email support.
2. **Pro Plan**: $79/month. Includes advanced analytics, priority support, and up to 5 user accounts.
3. **Enterprise Plan**: $199/month (or custom pricing). Includes dedicated account manager, unlimited users, and 24/7 phone support.

## Annual Subscriptions
We offer a 20% discount on all plans if billed annually.
